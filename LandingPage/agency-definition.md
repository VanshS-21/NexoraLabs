## 3. Why It Exists — The Problem

Most small businesses either have **no website**, or have one that looks unprofessional and doesn't convert visitors into customers.

They face three specific problems:

1. **Affordability** — Enterprise agencies are too expensive. Freelancers are too fragmented.
2. **Complexity** — They don't have the time or knowledge to coordinate a designer, a developer, and a strategist separately.
3. **Trust** — They've been burned by vendors who delivered something generic, late, or nothing at all.

Nexora Labs removes all three barriers. One team, one point of contact, one result.

## 4. How — The Delivery Process

A structured 5-step process that keeps the client informed without overwhelming them.

| Step | Phase | What Happens |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 1 | Discovery | A call to understand the business, goals, competitors, and budget |
| 2 | Strategy | Deliver a document with sitemap, content plan, and conversion goals |
| 3 | Design | Present 2 mockup directions, gather feedback, finalize |
| 4 | Build & Launch | Develop from a clean codebase, test, and go live within the agreed timeline |
| 5 | Handover | Client training, documentation, and optional ongoing retainer |

### Key Operating Principles

- **Client does not manage the process** — we do. They give input at defined checkpoints only.
- **Fixed scope per project** — clear deliverables, clear timeline, clear price.
- **One point of contact** — no back-and-forth between a designer and a developer the client has never met.
- **No templates. No shortcuts.** — every site is built from scratch, owned entirely by the client.

## 8. Illustrative Case Study

> *This is a fictional example of a typical Nexora Labs engagement. The client, results, and quotes are illustrative — representative of the problem and outcome this agency is built to deliver.*

### "From Zero to Fully Booked" — Nourish Skin Clinic, Pune

**Client:** Nourish Skin Clinic
**Industry:** Dermatology & aesthetics
**Location:** Pune, Maharashtra
**Business size:** 1 doctor, 2 staff, single clinic
**Engagement:** Growth Website (Tier 2) + Active Grow Retainer

---

### The Situation

Dr. Meera Joshi had been running Nourish Skin Clinic for three years on referrals alone. She had a Facebook page, a Justdial listing, and a WhatsApp number — but no website.

When a competitor opened two streets away with a polished website and online booking, she started losing new patients purely on first impressions. Her existing patients loved her. Google didn't know she existed.

She reached out through a local business WhatsApp group after another member posted about their experience with Nexora Labs.

**Her brief, paraphrased:**
> *"I want something clean and professional. I don't want it to look like a government hospital website. And I need people to be able to book an appointment without calling me."*

---

### The Problem We Diagnosed

After the discovery call, three issues were clear:

1. **Zero search visibility** — Nourish Skin Clinic had no Google presence. Searching "skin clinic Pune" returned her competitor on page one.
2. **No trust infrastructure** — New patients couldn't verify her credentials, see her work, or read reviews before calling.
3. **Friction in bookings** — Her only intake method was a phone call during clinic hours, which she frequently missed while with patients.

---

### What We Built

**Strategy deliverables:**
- 8-page sitemap: Home, About Dr. Meera, Treatments (4 sub-pages), Patient Stories, FAQs, Book Appointment
- Conversion goal defined per page — the homepage drives to "Book a Free Consultation"; each treatment page drives to a specific enquiry form
- Competitor audit of 3 local clinics — identified gaps in trust signals and mobile experience

**Design:**
- Presented 2 directions: clinical-minimal (white + sage green) and warm-premium (cream + terracotta)
- Client chose warm-premium
- Mobile-first — 70%+ of traffic expected on mobile
- Key trust elements: Dr. Meera's credentials prominently placed, before/after gallery (with consent disclaimers), patient testimonial section

**Development:**
- Custom-coded in Next.js — fast, SEO-friendly, and fully owned by the client
- Lightweight custom admin panel built for the clinic's specific content needs — update treatments, testimonials, and gallery without touching code
- Calendly embedded for appointment booking — zero friction, works 24/7
- Google Business Profile synced and optimised — address, hours, photos, category
- Schema markup for local medical business
- Page speed: 94/100 on mobile (Google PageSpeed)
- WhatsApp floating button on all pages
- Deployed on a VPS with automatic backups — no platform dependency, no monthly SaaS fees
